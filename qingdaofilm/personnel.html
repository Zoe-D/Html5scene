Folk Art Training Institutions

Qingdao also has a variety of folk art training institutions for different needs carrying out various types of flexible and diverse art training. Qingdao Radio and Television Arts and Crafts Art School sets up summer intensive training courses, performance classes (including film and television, opera performances, musical performances, etc.), director, choreographer class each year. Every weekend, there will be a film and television art classroom - Wanzai School in Oriental Movie Capital, which will invite professional organizations to provide free film and television courses for the public.

Informal Education

In the field of informal education, Qingdao Children's Palace regularly carry out a large number of film and television art-related training and education interaction. All kinds of schools also carry out a flexible and diverse second class and "Into the Movie" summer camp and film criticism activities. Municipal Bureau of Culture, the City Board of Education and other units regularly held "Excellent Film into the Campus" activities. More than 80 schools show the film 171 times each year, covering nearly 3 million students.
